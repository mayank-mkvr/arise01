About Us

ARISE is built by students, for students. We know how tough it gets to manage health, focus, and time during college life — so we created a simple tool that helps you track your activity in one place.

Our mission is to keep you on track with your goals using one clean, AI-powered tracker — so you can stay consistent, healthy, and productive every single day.
